Our Story

Hockey, Beer & Good People

Founded in 2007 by a group of HSG students who believed that the only thing better than a finance lecture was a fast game of hockey, the Gekkos have grown into one of Switzerland's most spirited student hockey clubs.

We compete in regional and national student tournaments, travel abroad for international games, and — most importantly — host the legendary Free Beer Game every semester.
