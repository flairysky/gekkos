Why Join?

The Best Investment You'll Make at HSG

Common Questions

You Asked, We Answered

Do I need to be good at hockey?

Absolutely not. We have players who had never laced up skates before joining us. Gordon Gekko himself probably couldn't skate, and look how far he got.

What does membership cost?

Membership fees are kept minimal. We'll share exact amounts in your welcome email. Equipment support is available for players who need it.

How many sessions per week?

We train twice a week during semester. Sessions are designed to fit around your academic schedule — because finance exams wait for no one.

What if I'm an exchange student?

Exchange students are fully welcome and a big part of what makes the Gekkos special. Short stays are fine — jump in for a semester.

Is the Free Beer Game really free?

Yes. Free. Beer. Game. Every semester. No catch. Show your HSG student card, get a beer, watch us play, cheer loud. It's that simple.

When does the next season start?

We recruit at the start of every semester. Apply now and we'll reach out ahead of the September kick-off with full onboarding details.
