Our Victories

Every title tells a story. Here's the complete Gekkos record.

🏆
2023
Zurich Winter Cup
Dominated the group stage and won the final 5–2. Captain Max Richter's hat-trick sealed the title in front of 300 fans.
🥇 Gold
🏆
2023
HSG Uni Cup
Home turf, home glory. A 4–1 win in the final over rivals from the University of Zurich cemented back-to-back titles.
🥇 Gold
🏆
2021
Inter-University Cup — Bern
Five-game winning streak without conceding more than 2 goals. A defensive masterclass capped by a 3–0 final victory.
🥇 Gold
🏆
2019
HSG Uni Cup
A golden period for the Gekkos. This title came after an undefeated group stage, with a commanding 6–2 final win.
🥇 Gold
🏆
2018
Basel Student Classic
Road victory. Playing on unfamiliar ice, the Gekkos showed resilience with a come-from-behind 4–3 overtime win in the final.
🥇 Gold
🏆
2015
Inter-University Cup
Our first major title. A landmark moment that transformed the Gekkos from a fun student club into a serious hockey team.
🥇 Gold
🥈
2022
Swiss Academic Hockey Championship
Runner-up finish in the most prestigious student hockey competition in Switzerland. Lost a tight final 3–2 after overtime.
🥈 Silver
🥈
2020
Zurich Winter Cup
Second place finish in a season shortened by the pandemic. The Gekkos gave everything in an extraordinary year.
🥈 Silver
🥉
2017
Lausanne Student Cup
Third place finish in our first visit to French-speaking Switzerland. A bronze that tasted like gold after two days of battles.
🥉 Bronze
🌟
2022
Best Team Spirit Award
Voted by tournament organizers for exceptional sportsmanship, crowd engagement, and for bringing the loudest fanbase in the building.
🏅 Special Award
🍺

Free Beer Game Tradition
Not an official trophy, but arguably our greatest achievement: 32 consecutive Free Beer Games. Never missed one. Never will.
🏅 Legendary

HSG Uni Cup
