Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — straightforward and transparent.

Last updated: June 2025

1. Who We Are

HSG Gekkos Hockey is the official ice hockey club of the University of St. Gallen (HSG), Switzerland. We are a non-profit student sports club. You can reach us at info@gekkoshockey.ch.

2. What Data We Collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us — for example, when you fill out our membership application form (name, email address, university programme, hockey experience). We do not use tracking cookies, analytics tools, or advertising networks on this website.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data submitted via the join form is used solely to process your membership application and to contact you about club activities, training schedules, and events. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Submitted form data is received via email and stored securely by the club board. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information. This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may collect anonymised server logs as part of their standard infrastructure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at info@gekkoshockey.ch. We will respond within 30 days.

6. External Links

This website contains links to external sites (Instagram, HSG, gekkoshockey.ch). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies separately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, please email us at info@gekkoshockey.ch or reach out via Instagram @hsg_gekkos.
